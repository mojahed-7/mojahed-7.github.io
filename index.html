02 — Key Finding

The weakest dimension is also the one that matters most

Medication explanation is the highest-leverage fix in the dataset

Across seven measurable dimensions of patient experience, medication explanation scores lowest nationally — and is the second most strongly correlated with a hospital's overall rating. Weak performance, high impact: the combination that should top any quality-improvement backlog.
